Ethereum Scratchies
Welcome to Ethereum Scratchies!

A transparent scratch lottery game on the Ethereum blockchain

How to play

Simply buy a ticket, wait for it to be mined and then scratch the cells to reveal their values. Reveal 3 or more matching values to win that amount of ether.

How it works

Each of the 12 cells on a ticket is converted to a unique and unpredictable hash of 32 digits. Each of these digits hold a value between 0 and 255. Cells are assigned an ether value based on how many of the first 5 digits of this hash are between 0 and 12.
